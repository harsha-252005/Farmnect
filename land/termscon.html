FarmNect Agreement - Terms and Conditions

Introduction

These Terms and Conditions govern the relationship between the Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, Government of India, and the parties entering into an agreement for land rental, lease, or product-related services.

Agreement Date and Price

  • All agreements must be made on a fixed date as agreed upon by both parties.
  • The price agreed upon must be fixed and shall not vary during the term of the agreement.
  • Payment terms and schedule must be clearly outlined and adhered to by both parties.

Land Rental and Lease Terms

  • The lease period shall be clearly defined in the agreement.
  • The land must be used strictly for the purposes agreed upon in the contract.
  • Any changes in land use must be approved in writing by both parties.
  • Both parties are responsible for maintaining the land in the agreed condition.

Product Agreements

  • All product-related agreements must specify the type, quantity, and quality of products.
  • Delivery schedules and conditions must be clearly defined and adhered to.
  • Any disputes related to product quality or delivery must be resolved according to the terms of the agreement.

General Terms

  • Both parties must adhere to all relevant local and national laws and regulations.
  • Any amendments to the agreement must be made in writing and signed by both parties.
  • In the event of a dispute, the matter shall be resolved through arbitration or as per the terms of the agreement.
  • This document is legally binding and enforceable under the laws of India.

Termination

  • Either party may terminate the agreement with a prior written notice as specified in the contract.
  • Termination without notice will result in penalties as outlined in the agreement.
  • All outstanding payments must be settled upon termination of the agreement.

Liability

  • Neither party shall be liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising out of the agreement.
  • The liability of each party shall be limited to the total amount paid or payable under the agreement.
  • Both parties must carry adequate insurance as required by law or specified in the agreement.

Governing Law

  • This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.
  • Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in India.
